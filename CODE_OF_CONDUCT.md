# 📜 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This is a beginner-friendly space. Everyone was a beginner once — even the people you look up to.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

---

## Our Standards

**The kind of behavior that makes this community great:**
- Being kind and patient with beginners
- Giving constructive, helpful feedback on PRs
- Celebrating others' contributions
- Asking questions openly without fear

**The kind of behavior that won't be tolerated:**
- Gatekeeping ("you should know this already")
- Dismissive or condescending comments
- Harassment of any kind
- Claiming someone else's work

---

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private issue or reaching out to the maintainer directly.

Consequences range from a warning to being permanently blocked from contributing.

---

## Remember

> "Every expert was once a beginner."

This project exists to give you a safe place to learn. Treat it that way.
